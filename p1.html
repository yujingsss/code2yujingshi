Modular Animation

Build with the concept of a modular animation with muptiple stages. Associate with visual design priciples of contrast, repetition and alignment.

Press mouse to trigger the next state of animation when the particles stop moving.

About

A modular animation depicting the movement of a particle. Consist only three colors, the particle starts with red color and bounces back to the middle as a white particle. This particle then spreds out to multiple particles bouncing around. When these white particles return to the middle again they will later align into tidy lines. The animation ends with all those particles return to their initial red particle state and dropping out of the sight.
